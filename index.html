Jamal Mamkhezri

Energy & Environmental Economist

I study how energy systems shape economies, environments, and communities. My research uses nonmarket valuation and experimental methods to understand public preferences for renewable energy policies and their real-world impacts.

About

I am a Kurd and take pride in my rich cultural heritage and resilience. We are the largest nation in the world without a state, yet our history, language, and traditions have endured for centuries.

Before pursuing economics, I was a competitive martial artist, becoming the Iranian national champion in kickboxing and full-contact in 2003, 2005, and 2006. I continue to practice and have taught martial arts in Albuquerque and mixed martial arts to NMSU students.

Outside of work, I enjoy fitness, spending time outdoors, and connecting with nature and animals—the wilder, the better.
